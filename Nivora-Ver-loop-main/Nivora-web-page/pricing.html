Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch plans anytime? expand_more

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll be charged the prorated difference. When downgrading, the credit will be applied to future billing cycles.

What's contextual memory? expand_more

Contextual memory allows Nivora to remember details from your past conversations, creating a more personalized and intelligent experience over time. It's like having an AI that truly knows you.

Is my data secure? expand_more

Absolutely. We use end-to-end encryption for all conversations. Your data is never used to train models, and you can export or delete your data at any time. Your intellectual sovereignty is paramount.

Do you offer refunds? expand_more

We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for all paid plans. If you're not satisfied, simply contact our support team within 14 days of your purchase for a full refund.
